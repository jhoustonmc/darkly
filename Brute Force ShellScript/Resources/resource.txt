Definition:

A brute force attack consists of attacker constantly spamming login details with random usernames and passwords in the hopes of finally guessing correctly

Exploitation:

1. Execute the shellscript "sh brute.sh", a basic brute force attack(Refer to definition).
2. If done correctly, the flag will appear in the terminal

Resolution:

Limit failed login attempts
Use Captcha
Limit logins to a specified IP address or range
Two factor authentication
Unique login URLs (does not stop a brute force attack but does make one more difficult)
Monitor server logs
